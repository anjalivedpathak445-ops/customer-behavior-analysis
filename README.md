# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior using **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to identify purchasing trends, customer preferences, spending habits, and business insights. The project includes data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based business queries, and an interactive Power BI dashboard for visualization.

The main objective is to understand customer purchasing behavior and generate meaningful insights that help improve business decision-making.

---

## Problem Statement
Businesses often struggle to understand customer buying patterns, spending habits, and product preferences. Manual analysis of large shopping datasets is difficult and time-consuming.

This project aims to analyze customer shopping behavior and answer important business questions such as:
- Which customer group generates more revenue?
- Which products are most purchased?
- Do subscribed customers spend more?
- How discounts affect customer purchases?
- What are customer buying trends?

---

## Objectives
- Analyze customer shopping behavior
- Identify top-performing products
- Compare spending patterns between customer groups
- Understand the impact of discounts on purchases
- Study customer subscription behavior
- Visualize shopping trends through dashboards

---

## Technologies Used
- **Python** → Data Cleaning & Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- **SQL** → Data Analysis & Business Queries
- **Power BI** → Interactive Dashboard & Visualization
- **Jupyter Notebook** → Analysis and preprocessing

---

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection
Collected customer shopping dataset containing:
- Customer information
- Product purchases
- Purchase amount
- Discounts
- Subscription details
- Review ratings
- Shipping types

### 2. Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
Performed:
- Missing value handling
- Data formatting
- Feature understanding
- Data preparation for analysis

### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Analyzed:
- Customer demographics
- Spending patterns
- Purchase distribution
- Product preferences
- Revenue analysis

### 4. SQL Analysis
Performed business-related queries such as:

- Revenue generated by male vs female customers
- Customers using discounts with high spending
- Top-rated products
- Purchase comparison between shipping methods
- Spending behavior of subscribed vs non-subscribed customers
- Discount impact on purchases
- Customer segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
- Top products in each category
- Repeat buyer subscription analysis
- Revenue contribution by age group

### 5. Dashboard Creation
Created an interactive **Power BI Dashboard** to visualize:
- Total Revenue
- Customer Segmentation
- Purchase Trends
- Product Performance
- Subscription Insights
- Discount Analysis

---

## Key Insights
- Customer spending behavior varies across demographics.
- Subscription status affects purchase patterns.
- Some products receive higher ratings and sales.
- Discounts significantly influence buying decisions.
- Loyal customers contribute more to overall revenue.
